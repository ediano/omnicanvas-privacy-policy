# Privacy Policy for OmniCanvas Privacy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

[**OmniCanvas Privacy** - Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/cofipknbkehhaildehfcljkedlbpcccp?utm_source=item-share-cb)

Your privacy is extremely important to us. The **OmniCanvas Privacy** browser extension is designed from the ground up to protect your visual privacy while using messaging web platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information.

---

## 1. Collection of Information
**OmniCanvas Privacy does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or sensitive information.** 

All operations performed by the extension happen locally on your device. We do not have servers, and we do not track your usage, messages, contacts, or analytics.

## 2. Permissions Used and Justification
To function correctly, the extension requires specific permissions within your browser. Here is why they are needed:
*   **`storage`:** Used exclusively to save your preferred privacy mode (Off, Full Blur, Hover, or Smart) locally on your device. This ensures your settings are remembered the next time you open your browser.
*   **Host Permissions (`https://web.whatsapp.com/*`, `https://web.telegram.org/*`, `https://www.instagram.com/*`):** Required solely to inject the visual blur effects (CSS) and manage the blur states (JavaScript) on these specific platforms. The extension cannot read, modify, or access your actual message content or account data.

## 3. Third-Party Data Sharing
Since we do not collect any data, **no data is ever sold, traded, or transferred to third parties.** Your communication remains strictly between you and the respective messaging platforms you use.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect structural changes in the extension. Any updates will be posted directly on this page with an updated revision date.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the extension's behavior, you can contact the publisher through the official Chrome Web Store support channel.
